##Commands:

- [go north] or [go n]
- [go west] or [go w]
- [go south] or [go s]
- [go east] or [go e]
- [look (the same way as above)]
- [open door (direction)]
- [close door (direction)]
- [quit]\n-[help]

##Move and open
You can open doors and close doors, if the door was close you can't move to next room.
To win the game you have to leave the "exit room".
